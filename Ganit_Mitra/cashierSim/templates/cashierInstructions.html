How to Play?

Start by clicking the 'Billing' button on our cashier machine

Then we can see a list of items and their prices, calculate the total and enter it in the white box. After that click enter

Correct input in total makes the Enter box green and the customer gives us some amount of Money.

Incorrect input in total makes the Enter box red, re-enter the correct amount to proceed!

Click on the images to give that amount of remainder cash return

Click Billing again to generate a ticket.

If correct amount of remainder sum was given u generate a green ticket

If incorrect amount of remainder sum was given u generate a red ticket and have to re-do everything

The game ends in 5 minutes, and is visible in the form of the green time bar on left. Once the bar is all red, GAME OVER!
