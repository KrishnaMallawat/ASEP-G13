Math Shape Catcher

Catch the right shapes to score points!

How to Play

  • Answer the math question to unlock the shape catching game.
  • Read the question and select the correct answer from the options.
  • After a correct answer, click "Start Catching Shapes!"
  • Move the catcher left/right with your mouse or finger to catch falling shapes.
  • Catch only the target shape shown at the top of the game area.
  • Each correct shape gives you points. Wrong shapes reduce your score.
  • Try to get the highest score before time runs out!
  • Click "Quit" anytime to return to the home screen.
