Case Study: Measurement

In a math class, students were given a project to measure different objects and convert units. They were provided with the following information:

  • Object 1: A rope is 5 meters long. Convert this length to centimeters.
  • Object 2: A bag of rice weighs 2.5 kilograms. Convert this weight to grams.
  • Object 3: A jug contains 3 liters of water. Convert this volume to milliliters.

The students were asked to perform the conversions and answer related questions.

Multiple Choice Questions

1. What is the length of the rope in centimeters?
2. What is the weight of the bag of rice in grams?
3. What is the volume of the jug in milliliters?
