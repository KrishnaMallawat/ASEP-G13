Case Study: Fractions and Decimals

Ananya is given a chocolate bar and breaks it into 4 equal pieces. She eats one piece. The fraction of the chocolate bar she ate is \( \frac{1}{4} \), and the fraction left is \( \frac{3}{4} \).

Multiple Choice Questions

1. What fraction of the chocolate bar did Ananya eat?
2.How many equal pieces was the chocolate bar divided into?
3. What is the fraction of the chocolate left?
4.What is the denominator of the fraction representing the chocolate bar?
5.What would the fraction be if Ananya ate two pieces?
