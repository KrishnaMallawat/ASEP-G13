Case Study: Area and Perimeter

A school is constructing a rectangular playground measuring 50 meters in length and 30 meters in width. A fence will be built around it, and a 5-meter-wide walking track will be added inside the playground along the perimeter.

Multiple Choice Questions

1.What is the total length of the fencing required around the playground?
2. What is the area of the playground?
3. What are the new dimensions of the playing area after removing the walking track?
4. What is the area covered by the walking track?
