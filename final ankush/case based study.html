Case-Based Study of Maths

How to Play

Welcome to Case-Based Study of Maths! This game is designed to test your problem-solving skills using real-world math scenarios. Here's how to play:

  1. Read the Problem: Each level presents a unique math problem based on real-life situations.
  2. Solve the Problem: Use your math skills to find the correct solution.
  3. Submit Your Answer: Enter your answer and see if you got it right!
  4. Earn Points: Correct answers earn you points. The faster you solve, the more points you get!
