Case Study: Fractions and Decimals

In a math class, students were given problems involving fractions and decimals. They were provided with the following tasks:

  • Task 1: Simplify the fraction \( \frac{12}{18} \).
  • Task 2: Convert the decimal \( 0.75 \) to a fraction.
  • Task 3: Add the fractions \( \frac{1}{4} \) and \( \frac{2}{5} \).

The students were asked to solve these problems and answer related questions.

Multiple Choice Questions

1. What is the simplified form of \( \frac{12}{18} \)?
2. What is the fraction form of the decimal \( 0.75 \)?
3. What is the sum of \( \frac{1}{4} \) and \( \frac{2}{5} \)?
