How to Play

Welcome to the Math Match Game! The objective of the game is to match each shape with its corresponding formula. Follow these steps to play:

  1. Click on a card to flip it and reveal its content.
  2. Try to find the matching card that pairs with the revealed card which gives the correct shape and formula.

  3. If the cards match, they will disappear. If not, they will flip back.
  4. Continue until all pairs are matched.

  5. Enjoy the celebration when you complete the game!
